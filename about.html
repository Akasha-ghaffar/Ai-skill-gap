About Our Project

AI Skill Gap Finder is a web-based management system developed to bridge the divide between current workforce capabilities and the demands of the modern era. It provides secure authentication, comprehensive user registration, and intelligent gap analysis for professionals seeking to stay ahead in the tech-driven landscape.

Project Features

Skill Mapping

Securely log and validate your current skills and qualifications to receive personalized gap analysis and growth paths.

AI Job Match

Enter your professional profile to receive personalized AI job recommendations tailored to your unique expertise.

CV Generator

Automatically generate a professional, high-impact CV based on your verified skills and academic experience.
